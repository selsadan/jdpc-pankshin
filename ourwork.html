Our Work

Animation and Research

Our focus is on building the capacity of communities for collaboration and solidarity to enhance community-driven development owned by the people. We engage on training of our staff and community stakeholders in various aspects of social development to equip them with contemporary tools in development for improved productivity and expertise as well as enquiry to identify basic community needs. Some activities under this theme are:

  • Sensitization and Awareness Creation
  • Resource Mobilization
  • Capacity Building
  • Establishment and Monitoring of Community Structures
​

Social Welfare

We have the mandate to investigate and assess pressing human needs and organize human and material resources for support and also to support traumatized groups with skills for better recovery. In the same way, we attend to the needs of disadvantaged groups. The following are some activities we engage in:

  • Promoting Micro Enterprise (PROMIC)
  • Emergency Relief Intervention
  • Psychosocial Support Services
  • Identification of Early Warning Signs and prompt reporting
  • Training and Establishment of Emergency Preparedness and Response Teams
  • Training and Establishment of Peace Clubs
  • Alternative to Violence Project (AVP)
  • Peace Building and Conflict Mitigation

Good Governance and Human Right

The organization organize and implement programmes that promote accountability in governance through civic and political education, town hall meetings and promoting gender inclusion in decision making. We also promote and protect the dignity of the marginalized, oppressed and unjustly treated people in the society through legal aid clinic and support. Some of the activities carried out in this area are

  • Civic, Voter and Political Education Trainings
  • Community sensitization and Civic and Political Education
  • Constituency Town Hall Meetings
  • Constituency Round Table Discussions
  • Election Observation
  • Legal Aid Clinic
  • Correctional Service Centre Apostolate
  • Community Sensitization of Basic Human Rights

Urban and Rural Development

Our goal here is to support communities to have access to reliable sources of portable water supply, improved health status and to promote an echo – friendly environment by means of enhancing climate change adaptation measures in communities. Some activities of this area are:

  • Trainings on Community Led Total Sanitation (CLTS)
  • Digging of Wells in Communities
  • Constructions of Boreholes
  • Sensitization of the used of Improved Cook Stoves
  • Sensitization of the use of Urine Fertilizers
  • Water Sanitation and Hygiene (WaSH) components in communities
  • Sensitization on sustainable crop and livestock production
  • Awareness creation on Climate Change and Environmental Care
  • Capacity Strengthening on food processing and skill acquisition
